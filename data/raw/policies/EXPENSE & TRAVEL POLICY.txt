EXPENSE & TRAVEL POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Version: 1.0

SALES TEAM EXPENSE GUIDELINES

Client Entertainment:
- Up to $150 per client per month
- Requires itemized receipt
- Must be business-related (meals, events, activities)
- Alcohol limited to $50 per event
- No entertainment for internal team members

Travel - Airfare:
- Economy airfare for trips under 5 hours
- Business class requires VP approval for trips over 5 hours
- Manager approval required for trips over $800
- Book at least 2 weeks in advance for best rates
- Use company-approved travel vendors

Travel - Ground Transportation:
- Rental car: Economy class, pre-approved vendors only
- Taxi/Uber: Standard service only (no premium options)
- Parking: Reimbursed at actual cost with receipt
- Tolls: Reimbursed at actual cost with receipt

Mileage Reimbursement:
- $0.67 per mile for personal vehicle use on client visits
- Must track mileage with start/end locations
- Reimbursed monthly with mileage log
- Personal vehicle insurance must be valid

Hotel Accommodations:
- Standard room rate (no suites)
- Up to $200/night in major cities
- Up to $150/night in other areas
- Manager approval required for rates above limits

Meals & Per Diem:
- Breakfast: $15 (if traveling before 6 AM)
- Lunch: $20
- Dinner: $35
- Total daily limit: $60 (without client entertainment)
- Receipts required for expenses over $25

Home Office Stipend:
- $50/month for fully remote sales team members
- One-time setup allowance: $200 for equipment
- Covers internet, phone, utilities portion
- Paid via monthly expense reimbursement

Expense Submission:
- Submit within 30 days of expense
- Include itemized receipts for all expenses
- Categorize by type (travel, entertainment, mileage, etc.)
- Manager approval required before reimbursement
- Finance processes within 10 business days

Prohibited Expenses:
- Personal entertainment (movies, books, gym)
- Alcoholic beverages (except client entertainment)
- Traffic violations or parking tickets
- Personal grooming or clothing
- Commuting to home office
- Family member expenses

Approved by: VP Sales Operations
