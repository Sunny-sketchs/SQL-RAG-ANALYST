DISCOUNT APPROVAL POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Version: 1.0

DISCOUNT AUTHORIZATION LIMITS

Sales Rep Authority:
- Sales reps may apply discounts up to 15% on any order without approval.

Regional Manager Authority:
- Discounts between 15% and 25% require sign-off from the regional sales manager, 
  logged in the order notes field.

Finance & VP Authority:
- Discounts above 25% require Finance approval and are capped at 40% 
  except for end-of-quarter clearance sales, which may go up to 50% with VP approval.

Commission Impact:
- Any order with discount_applied greater than 20% is excluded from standard 
  commission calculations (see Commission Policy) and is instead reviewed under 
  the Finance Adjustment process.

Documentation Requirements:
- All discounts must be logged in the order notes field
- Discounts 15%+ require manager/finance sign-off
- Discounts 25%+ require Finance approval with business justification
- End-of-quarter clearance sales 40%+ require VP approval

Approval Workflow:
1. Sales Rep applies discount ≤15% → Auto-approved
2. Sales Rep applies discount 15-25% → Regional Manager review → Approved/Rejected
3. Sales Rep applies discount 25-40% → Finance review → Approved/Rejected
4. Sales Rep applies discount 40-50% (Q-end clearance) → VP review → Approved/Rejected
5. Any discount >50% → Rejected (not permitted)

Approved by: VP Sales Operations
