COMMISSION POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Version: 1.0

COMMISSION TIERS BY QUOTA ATTAINMENT

Base Commission Rate:
- 3% of net sales (after discount) for all sales teams

Tier Structure (based on quarterly quota attainment):
- Below 80% attainment: Base rate only (3%)
- 80-99% attainment: Base rate + 0.5% bonus (3.5% total)
- 100-124% attainment: Base rate + 1.5% bonus (4.5% total)
- 125%+ attainment ("Accelerated Tier"): Base rate + 3% bonus (6% total)

Regional Modifier:
- Teams in the West region receive an additional 0.5% on top of their tier rate, 
  due to higher cost-of-living adjustments.

Exclusions:
- Commission is calculated on net sales after discount_applied.
- Orders with a discount above 20% are excluded from commission-eligible revenue 
  and require Finance review (see Discount Approval Policy).

Calculation Example:
- Sales Rep in West region with 110% quota attainment
- Gross Sales: $100,000
- Discount Applied: 10%
- Net Sales: $90,000
- Commission Rate: 4.5% (base 3% + 1.5% bonus) + 0.5% (West modifier) = 5%
- Commission Earned: $90,000 × 5% = $4,500
