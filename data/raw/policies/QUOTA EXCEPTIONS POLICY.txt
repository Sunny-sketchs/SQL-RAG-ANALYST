QUOTA EXCEPTIONS POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Version: 1.0

NEW HIRE AND EXCEPTION QUOTA RULES

New Sales Team Member Ramp Schedule:
- Month 1-2: 50% of standard team quota
- Month 3-4: 75% of standard team quota
- Month 5+: 100% of standard team quota

Example:
- Standard team quota: $100,000/month
- New hire Month 1: $50,000 quota
- New hire Month 3: $75,000 quota
- New hire Month 5: $100,000 quota

Mid-Quarter Team Member Loss:
- Teams that lost a member mid-quarter receive a pro-rated quota reduction 
  equal to (days without replacement / total days in quarter) × standard quota, 
  applied at quarter-end reconciliation.

Example:
- Standard team quota: $300,000/quarter (90 days)
- Team member leaves on day 30 (60 days remaining without replacement)
- Quota reduction: (60 / 90) × $300,000 = $200,000
- Adjusted team quota: $300,000 - $200,000 = $100,000

Regional Market Disruption Exceptions:
- Teams in regions affected by a documented market disruption 
  (e.g., a named regional event) may apply for a one-time quota adjustment 
  via the Sales Operations exception form.

Eligible Disruptions:
- Natural disasters (hurricanes, earthquakes, floods)
- Major economic events (market crashes, industry downturns)
- Infrastructure failures (power outages, internet outages)
- Regulatory changes affecting the region
- Major competitor actions or market consolidation

Exception Request Process:
1. Sales manager submits exception form to Sales Operations
2. Form includes: disruption description, impact analysis, quota adjustment request
3. Sales Operations reviews with Finance and VP Sales
4. Decision made within 5 business days
5. Adjustment applied retroactively to quarter start if approved
